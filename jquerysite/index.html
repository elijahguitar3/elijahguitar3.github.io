Quick Time Tacos!

Serves 3-4 people

Looking for resturant quality tacos in your own home?

In less than 20 minutes, you can make them yoursel!

Save a trip to Taco Bell, make Quick Time Tacos!

Ingredients

  • Seasoned ground beef
  • Hard corn taco shells
  • Shreaded cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Lettuce
  • Diced tomato
  • Pre-made spanish rice
  • Hot sauce

Cook the Meat

Thoroughly cook the ground beef in a pan on stove top. <
This will take 5-10 minutes". Mix in hot sauce to taste.

Filling the Tacos with Meat

Place the hard taco shells on a plate, with the opening facing up. <
Use a spoon to fill the tacos with the seasoned ground beef you just prepared.


Top Your Tacos!

Add sour cream, shredded cheddar cheese, lettuce, diced tomatoes, and hot sauce to the top of your taco. Add or skip any toppings you may want.
