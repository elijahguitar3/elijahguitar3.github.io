Why I Do It...

I have many passions in my life but none as great as my passion for music. It is the last thing on my mind when I fall asleep, the first thing on my mind in the morning and it wakes me up in the middle of the night. Working in music sets my soul at rest and my heart on fire.

My Latest Adventure

"Mary" | an Opera by Kanye West | Art Basel 2020 | Miami FL

During Art Basel Miami, 2020, I was hired by III Points in association with Space to help manage several events. I was first hired to help orchestrate the exclusive merch release for ASAP Rocky’s “Midnite Rave” in Wynwood, FL. After this event, I was hired to work an exciting, spontaneous event. Kanye West decided to release his new opera, Mary, during Art Basel, less than 48 hours before show time. The event was held at the Miami Marine Stadium on Virginia Key- a historic Miami land mark. My duties for this event included coordinating security operations and handling concierge for the guest list. It was a wonderful show and an Amazing experience.
